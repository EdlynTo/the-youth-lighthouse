About Us

Canada is renowned for its intake of newcomers yearly. However, with 500,000 people coming in each year, support is bound to be a lacking resource, especially for young girls. Established in 2024, our mission is to support girls as they adapt to their new surroundings and to empower them to reach their full potential by helping cross obstacles faced daily and providing them with opportunities to support their ambitions.
